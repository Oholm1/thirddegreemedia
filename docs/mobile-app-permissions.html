How App Permissions Leak You Mobile Privacy Guide

This is a short, practical guide explaining common mobile permissions (location, microphone, camera, storage, contacts) and the privacy risks they pose. Grant permissions only when needed, prefer one-time or while using access, and audit apps regularly.

Quick checklist

  • Review apps with Location, Microphone, Camera and Storage access.
  • Revoke background location for apps that don't need it.
  • Uninstall apps you don't use.
  • Use one-time permissions when available.
