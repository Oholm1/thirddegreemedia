🧰 NoScript — Script Control for Power Users

NoScript blocks JavaScript and other active content by default, letting you whitelist only the domains you trust. It’s open source and maintained by Giorgio Maone.

  • Granular control: Per-site allow/deny for scripts, objects, media.
  • Hardening extras: Anti-XSS and clickjacking protections.
  • Pairs well: Use alongside uBlock Origin for network-level blocking.

Heads-up: It will break many modern sites until you allow just the minimal domains needed. This is for people who enjoy tuning.

Get it: noscript.net (Firefox/Chromium).

🐧 Why Linux Deserves Your Attention

Linux isn’t just an operating system—it’s a movement. Built on the principles of openness, collaboration, and user freedom, Linux powers everything from smartphones and servers to supercomputers and satellites. Whether you're a developer, a privacy enthusiast, or just someone who wants to break free from corporate ecosystems, Linux offers a customizable, secure, and endlessly fascinating alternative.

🔓 What Makes Linux Special

  • Open Source: You can view, modify, and redistribute the code. No gatekeepers.
  • Community-Driven: Thousands of contributors worldwide keep it evolving.
  • Privacy-Respecting: No telemetry, no tracking—just raw control.
  • Lightweight & Fast: Perfect for older machines or high-performance setups.
  • Secure by Design: Built with multi-user architecture and strong permission systems.

🧠 Popular Linux Distributions

  • Ubuntu – User-friendly and great for beginners.
  • Arch Linux – Minimalist and customizable for power users.
  • Fedora – Cutting-edge features with Red Hat backing.
  • Kali Linux – Tailored for cybersecurity and ethical hacking.
