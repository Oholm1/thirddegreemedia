Signal — Private Messaging That Just Works

End-to-end encrypted messages, calls, and groups. Open-source apps, modern protocols, and sane defaults—no ads, no trackers.

Updated: 2025-09-10

Why Signal

  • E2EE by default for 1:1, groups, voice/video.
  • Open-source clients with active audits and community scrutiny.
  • Modern privacy features: disappearing messages, view-once media, screen security, registration lock.
  • Minimal metadata: designed to keep as little as possible.

Install

  • Mobile: iOS App Store / Google Play (Signal Foundation).
  • Desktop: macOS / Windows / Linux (pairs with your phone).

You register with a phone number; you can hide it from non-contacts and limit who can find you.

Recommended settings (2 minutes)

  • Privacy → enable Registration Lock (PIN), Screen Security, and restrict who can find you by number.
  • Safety Numbers → verify for sensitive contacts (tap name → View Safety Number).
  • Disappearing messages → set a sensible default (e.g., 1 week) per chat.
  • Calls → enable Always Relay Calls if you prefer hiding IPs (may reduce quality).

Verify it’s private

  1. Open a chat → compare Safety Numbers in person or via another channel.
  2. Send a disappearing message and confirm it vanishes on both devices.
  3. Desktop pairing: confirm the “Linked Devices” list only shows your machines.

Get Signal
