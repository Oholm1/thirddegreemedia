Contribute

Third Degree Media is a curated space for people who care about privacy, digital sovereignty, censorship resistance, and free speech. If you align with those values, I’d love to consider your work.

What to Send

  • Original essays, technical deep dives, reporting, op-eds, or guides.
  • Format as Markdown (.md) (preferred), plain text, or LibreOffice (.odt).
  • Attach images only if you hold rights or they’re licensed for reuse (include attribution).

Tip: Start from the article template.

How to Submit

  1. Email your piece to contribute@thirddegreemedia.com (or use the PGP option below).
  2. Include the name or pseudonym you want to publish under.
  3. Add one-line bio + relevant links (optional).

Prefer privacy? Encrypt with my PGP key (below) and send the ciphertext in the email body or as an attachment.

Editorial Policy

  • This is a curated publication. All submissions are reviewed. Publishing is at my discretion.
  • I may lightly edit for clarity, grammar, and house style. Substantive changes are confirmed with you first.
  • Anonymous bylines are possible; I’ll need a way to contact you during editing.

Legal & Safety

  • Do not submit illegal content of any kind (e.g., doxxing/PII leaks, copyrighted material without rights, exploitation, incitement).
  • By submitting, you affirm the work is yours to publish and you grant Third Degree Media a non-exclusive right to publish it on the site (you retain your copyright).
  • Citations and claims: include sources. For investigations, keep notes handy; I may ask for verification.
  • We minimize data collection and keep submissions via email—no trackers, no web forms.
